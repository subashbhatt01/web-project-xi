Information Security

Information security is the practice of protecting information by mitigating information risks. It is part of information risk management and involves identifying and applying information security industry standards as mechanisms of protection and prevention.

Information security attributes include confidentiality, integrity, and availability (CIA). Confidentiality refers to ensuring that information is only accessible to authorized individuals or systems. Integrity refers to ensuring that information is accurate and complete. Availability refers to ensuring that information is accessible when needed.
